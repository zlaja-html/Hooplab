Success story

Xavier Boyd used the pathway to move up.

After arriving in Germany, Xavier joined the Boeblingen Panthers and used that platform to adapt, earn minutes, and prove his value in a structured club environment.

With consistent performance, targeted exposure, and active communication in the German basketball market, interest from higher-level clubs followed. After half a season, Xavier secured a move to Kirchheim, a Regional League program.

FAQ

Where is HoopLab Agency based?

HoopLab Agency is based in Boeblingen, Germany and works with players who want basketball development, scouting, tryouts, and exposure opportunities across Europe.

How do HoopLab basketball tryouts work?

Tryouts are evaluation-based. Coaches look at consistency, decision-making, coachability, effort, and performance under pressure rather than empty drills alone.

Can I book individual basketball workouts in Germany?

Yes. When workout slots are open, players can book through the site and train in sessions built around film review, development gaps, and practical game transfer.

Who are the group sessions for?

Group sessions are for players who want competitive reps, direct coaching, and a small-group basketball training environment instead of generic open-gym runs.
